About InteractDNS

InteractDNS is a free alternative to Burp Collaborator that uses the powerful Interactsh service to detect out-of-band interactions. It's perfect for testing SQL injections, SSRF, XXE, and other vulnerabilities that trigger DNS lookups.

All interactions are stored locally in your browser and never transmitted to any third-party services. Interactsh domains are generated on-demand and are valid for a limited time.
